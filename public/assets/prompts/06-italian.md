# Relish — Italian Fiesta Dish Images
> Category: `italian` | IDs: `ita-001` through `ita-006`
> Reference `00-style-guide.md` for BASE STYLE, colour palette, and full negative prompts.

Each dish has two prompts:
- **Thumbnail** — 64 × 64 px, 1:1, WebP — used in `MenuCard` (`w-16 h-16`)
- **Detail Hero** — 430 × 322 px, 4:3, WebP — used in `ItemDetail` (`aspect-[4/3]`)

All pasta and risotto dishes use a **wide shallow white ceramic bowl or flat rimmed pasta plate**
with the double rim: outer `#8B1024` red, inner `#D9A03A` gold. Pizza uses a round flat plate.
