# Relish — Desserts Dish Images
> Category: `desserts` | IDs: `des-001` through `des-006`
> Reference `00-style-guide.md` for BASE STYLE, colour palette, and full negative prompts.

Each dish has two prompts:
- **Thumbnail** — 64 × 64 px, 1:1, WebP — used in `MenuCard` (`w-16 h-16`)
- **Detail Hero** — 430 × 322 px, 4:3, WebP — used in `ItemDetail` (`aspect-[4/3]`)

Desserts use a **slightly warmer, more intimate light** than savoury dishes — key light still
from upper-left at ~3200 K but with a touch more amber for an after-dinner indulgence feel.
All plates retain the double rim: outer `#8B1024` red, inner `#D9A03A` gold.
