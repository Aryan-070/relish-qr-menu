# Relish — Category Banner Images
> Applies to: `CategoryPage` hero banner for each of the 5 categories
> Reference `00-style-guide.md` for BASE STYLE, colour palette, and negative prompts.

All banners: **CSS 430 × 184 px → Generate at 2×: 860 × 369 px | Aspect: 21:9 | Format: WebP**
Angle: Wide 20° overhead, landscape composition, hero dish centred, secondary props flanking.
The CategoryPage renders these at full width above the item list — they must read clearly
even at small screen widths and should not have any text baked in (text is overlaid by the app).
