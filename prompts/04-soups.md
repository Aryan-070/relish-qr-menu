# Relish — Soups Dish Images
> Category: `soups` | IDs: `soup-001` through `soup-005`
> Reference `00-style-guide.md` for BASE STYLE, colour palette, and full negative prompts.
> **NEW:** the shallow white bowl now carries the gold `Relish` serif wordmark on its lower rim. See "Brand Wordmark" in `00-style-guide.md`.

Each dish has two prompts:
- **Thumbnail** — 64 × 64 px, 1:1, WebP — used in `MenuCard` (`w-16 h-16`)
- **Detail Hero** — 430 × 322 px, 4:3, WebP — used in `ItemDetail` (`aspect-[4/3]`)

All soups are served in a **wide shallow white ceramic bowl** — diameter fills 80% of square (thumbnail) or 65% of width (hero). Bowl has the double rim: outer `#8B1024` red, inner `#D9A03A` gold.
